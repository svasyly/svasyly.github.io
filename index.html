About Me

I am a 6th year Ph.D Student in the Astronomy Department at the University of California, Berkeley.

My research primarily focuses on follow-up of astrophysical transients, from supernovae to gravitational wave sources.

Research

Current Research

  • Applications of polarimetry in time domain astrophysics
  • Ultraviolet and optical spectroscopy of Type II supernovae
